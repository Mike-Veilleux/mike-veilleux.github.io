About me

I’ve lived in many countries and worked with many different people and technologies. I embrace diversity and thrive in bringing men and technologie together to create great software solutions.

I'm now based in Hong Kong for the last six years and working in the education. I've been teaching and supporting students on various project using Autodesk products, WebGL and Virtual Reality with Unity3D, microcontroller and many more. I’ve recently joined the college ICT team as database analyst and full stack developer.
