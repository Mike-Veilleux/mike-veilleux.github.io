I grew up coding on commodore computers in the 80’s, spent twenty years working on Computer Aided Design software solutions. My last six years were in the education sector teaching and developing for Autodesk, Unity3D and Arduino. I’m now fully focused on web based development using the lateste stack such as NodeJS and React. I’ve lived in many countries and worked with many different people, culture, sectors and technologies. I embrace diversity and thrive in bringing men and machines together to make our world a better place to live in.
